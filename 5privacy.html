If you decide that we should not use your Personal Information to contact you, please let us know and we will not use that information for such purposes.

6. Cookies

Uproute uses “cookies”. A cookie is a small data file that a website can transfer to a visitor’s hard drive to keep records of the visits to such site. A cookie contains information such as your username and password that helps us recognize the pages you have visited and improve future visits, but the only personal information a cookie can contain is the information that you provide yourself. A cookie cannot read data off of your hard drive or read cookie files created by other sites. If you prefer not to accept a cookie you can set your web browser to warn you before accepting cookies or refusing all cookies. Access to some of our secured websites may require the use of cookies, which you must enable. You should see your web browser “help” for instructions on how to enable, disable or limit the sites from which you accept cookies. Otherwise, you can still access most of the features on our website without accepting a cookie.
